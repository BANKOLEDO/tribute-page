Hokage Uzumaki Naruto

The Man Who Saved The Hidden Leaf Village

This is the Subject Of The Tribute Page

Introduction

  • Naruto Uzumaki is introduced as a young orphan boy of 12 years with blond, spiky hair and blue eyes.
  • Graduates as a ninja from Konohagakure while bonding with his teacher Iruka Umino.

Early Life and Motivations

  • Naruto seeks attention due to being ridiculed during his childhood.
  • Resolves to become Konohagakure's Hokage to be accepted and respected.
  • Aims to surpass all previous leaders despite difficulties.

Ninja Journey

  • Forms friendships he initially lacked, some likened to family relationships.
  • Despite occasional failures in his tasks, other characters believe in his potential as an excellent Hokage due to his positive impact on their lives.

Adulthood and Family

  • Claims Konohagakure village as his family because of his role as the new Hokage, a lesson learned from the Third Hokage Hiruzen Sarutobi.
  • Suffers a poor relationship with his son, Boruto, due to spending little time with his bloodline family.
